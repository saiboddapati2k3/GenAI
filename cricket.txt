Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of eleven players.
Originating in England, it has become a global phenomenon, especially in countries like India, 
Australia, and England. The game is played in various formats, including Test matches, One Day
Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20) games. Each team takes turns batting 
and bowling, aiming to score more runs than the opponent. Cricket demands a mix of 
strategy, skill, and teamwork. Iconic players like Sachin Tendulkar, Virat Kohli, 
and M.S. Dhoni have left lasting legacies. With passionate fans and thrilling
matches, cricket continues to unite and excite people worldwide.
